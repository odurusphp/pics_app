About Us.

Our management and team of expertise are very competent young dynamic and in their professional prime. The team is made up of Ghanaian professionals and associated expatriate staffs, trained and worked in the United States of America, Europe and Africa, in their respective fields of Energy & Power, Banking, Mining & Construction, Telecommunication, Environment Assessment, Management information systems, Project management, Project Monitoring & Evaluation and General Consultancy industries.

Our team of advisers and professional are well welded into a dynamic multi-disciplinary unit, with the capacity to produce timely and innovative but practical solutions to complex project development, project management, environmental Impact assessment, corporate/financial problems and/or issues.

Our business target areas in the Ghana and other West Africa countries include the provision of the following consultancy services:
  • Project/Business Development, Monitoring and Evaluation;
  • Project/Business Finance Arrangement and Project Fund Micro-management;
  • Local Agency and Office Representative of Expatriate Investors and Multinational Companies with business interest in Ghana and other West African countries;
  • General Investments Banking services (acquisition & mergers, company valuation, Pre-investments study, etc.);
  • Environmental and Social Assessment services;
  • Training and Complete Human Capital Sourcing, Management and Evaluation;
  • Commodity Trading and Financing Advisory Services; and
  • Dividers
  • General Finance, Investments and Management Advisory Services.
